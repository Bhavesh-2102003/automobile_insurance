Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in my roadside assistance coverage?

All our Comprehensive Coverage plans include basic roadside assistance (towing, jump starts, lockout service) up to 4 incidents per year. Higher-tier plans offer additional benefits like longer towing distances and rental car coverage.

How quickly can I expect help to arrive?

Our average response time is 32 minutes in urban areas and 45 minutes in rural areas. Response times may vary based on location and weather conditions.

Can I use roadside assistance for someone else's vehicle?

Yes, your coverage follows you as the policyholder, not the vehicle. You can request assistance when you're driving any vehicle, including rental cars.
