Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between comprehensive and third-party coverage?

Comprehensive coverage protects your own vehicle against damage from accidents, theft, and natural disasters, while third-party coverage protects you against claims made by others for injury or damage you cause.

Is zero depreciation worth the extra cost?

Zero depreciation is especially valuable for new cars (0-3 years old) as it ensures you receive the full claim amount without deduction for wear and tear, making it cost-effective in case of major repairs.

Can I switch between coverage options?

Yes, you can change your coverage options at renewal time. Our advisors can help you determine the best combination of coverage for your needs and budget.
